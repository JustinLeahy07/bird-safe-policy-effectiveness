# Policy Excerpt — New York City, NY
## Local Law 15 of 2020 (Bird-Friendly Materials)

SOURCE:
nyc_ny_ll15_2020_official_text.pdf

OFFICIAL URL:

DATE EXCERPTED:
2025-12-28

---

## Applicability / Scope
This law applies to new buildings and alterations to existing buildings where an exterior wall envelope is constructed or replaced. Requirements apply to exterior walls, including glazed areas, up to a height of seventy-five (75) feet above grade.

---

## Trigger / Threshold
Bird-friendly material requirements are triggered when a building project involves the construction or replacement of exterior walls or glazing within the regulated height zone. The seventy-five foot height threshold establishes the vertical extent of required compliance.

---

## Glass / Façade Treatment Requirements
The law requires the use of bird-friendly materials on exterior wall surfaces. Bird-friendly materials are defined as materials that reduce reflectivity or transparency in order to mitigate bird collisions. The law also addresses hazardous features, including glass corners and fly-through conditions.

---

## Lighting Restrictions
No lighting restrictions are specified in this law.

---

## Compliance / Review Mechanism
Compliance with bird-friendly material requirements is reviewed as part of the Department of Buildings permit approval process. Construction documents must demonstrate compliance with the bird-friendly material standards.

---

## Enforcement / Penalties
Failure to comply with the requirements of this law constitutes a violation subject to enforcement by the Department of Buildings under applicable provisions of the New York City Administrative Code.

---

## Exceptions / Waivers
No explicit exemptions or waivers are specified within the law text.
