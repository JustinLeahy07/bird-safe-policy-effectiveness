## Purpose of Guidance
This document provides interpretive guidance for designers and applicants to clarify how to comply with the bird-friendly material requirements established under Local Law 15 of 2020.

---

## Applicability / Scope Clarification
The guidance clarifies that compliance is required for applicable new construction and alteration projects involving exterior wall envelopes within seventy-five feet above grade, and explains how the requirements apply to various façade conditions.

---

## Definitions
Bird-friendly materials are defined as materials that reduce bird collisions through visible patterning, reduced reflectivity, or other design features. Hazardous features include transparent or reflective glazing and architectural conditions that create fly-through illusions.

---

## Compliance Documentation
Applicants must submit construction documents demonstrating compliance with bird-friendly material standards. Documentation may include façade drawings, material specifications, and certification statements submitted during permit review.

---

## Enforcement / Review
Compliance is verified by the Department of Buildings during plan review and inspection phases associated with building permit approval.

---

## Notes
This guidance document operationalizes Local Law 15 by translating statutory language into actionable compliance steps for applicants and reviewers.
